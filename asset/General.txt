General

This game is about how you lead and manage your village, collect resources, 
and become the number 1. You are provided with many features that'll make 
you enjoy while playing this game. There are some mechanics that you need 
to know before playing this game.

You'll live in a village in a maze that is designed to make your village 
more protected since the attacker must determine to travel the maze and
your defense can attack those attackers. 

But you can attack another village too with the troops provided by this
game. but there's a troop capacity that your camp can handle so you must 
be wise to manage your troop.

If you want to know more, you can click the buttons on the sidebar.
